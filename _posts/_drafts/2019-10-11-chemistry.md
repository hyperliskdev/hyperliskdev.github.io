## The Atom
The atom is mostly empty space with a small dense positive nucleus with negative electrons spinning around the outside in quantized orbits.

### Weights of subatomic particles < +, - and = >

Protons <+> = 1.0072766u <br>
Neutrons <=> = 1.0086654u <br>
Electrons <-> = 0.00001u

## Atomic Mass

Isotopes: an element that has the same number of protons but differing number of neutrons. 

Radioisotope: isotope with an unstable nucleus.

Isotopic abundance: The percentage of a particular isotope of an element as it exists in nature.

### Calculating average atomic mass
Atomic mass is calculated by abundance compared to actual weight. Since there are multiple "versions" of carbon (ie, Carbon-13 or Carbon-14). The masses have to be calculated based on the abundance of each isotope.

Chlorine = (34.97u * 0.7578) + (36.97u * 0.2422) = 35.4544u <br>
(Hint: the decimal number without a one's place is actually a percentage...)
